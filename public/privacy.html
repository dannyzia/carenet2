CareNet Privacy Policy & Data Safety

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

CareNet collects personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you create an account to facilitate caregiving services. We also collect photos if you choose to upload profile pictures or payment proofs. We securely track billing and CarePoint transactions to ensure the app functions correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide the CareNet service, manage user accounts, verify payments, and facilitate communication between Guardians and Care Providers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Security & Encryption

All data transmitted between the CareNet app and our servers is encrypted in transit using secure HTTPS protocols. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your account and financial data.

4. User Data Deletion Instructions

How to request deletion of your account and data:

If you wish to delete your account and all associated personal data from CareNet, you can do so by contacting our support team.

Please send an email to admin@carenet.digital-papyrus.xyz with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account.

Our team will verify your identity, process your request, and permanently delete your account, personal details, and uploaded media within 7 business days.
