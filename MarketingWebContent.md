## How to Use the App Page

### Page Header

**Headline:** Getting Started with CareNet

**Description:** Your complete guide to using CareNet Bangladesh. Find answers to common questions and learn how to make the most of our platform.

---

### Getting Started

#### Step 1: Choose Your Role
Select your role when registering—Guardian, Caregiver, Agency, Patient, or Shop Owner. Each role has a tailored experience designed for your specific needs.

#### Step 2: Create Your Account
Provide your basic information including name, phone number, and email. Verify your phone number to secure your account and enable two-factor authentication.

#### Step 3: Complete Your Profile
Fill in your profile details based on your role:
- **Guardians:** Add patients you care for and set up your preferences
- **Caregivers:** Upload certifications, build your portfolio, and set your availability
- **Agencies:** Provide license details, add your services, and onboard your team
- **Patients:** Add health information, emergency contacts, and preferences
- **Shop Owners:** Set up your shop, add products, and configure shipping

#### Step 4: Start Using CareNet
Begin using the features designed for your role. Explore your dashboard, browse available options, and start connecting with the CareNet community.

---

### For Guardians: How to Find and Book Care

#### 1. Post a Care Requirement
Navigate to "New Care Requirement" and fill in the details:
- Patient information (age, condition, care needs)
- Schedule requirements (days, hours, start date)
- Location
- Budget range
- Special requirements (language, skills, equipment)

#### 2. Review Agency Bids
Wait for agencies to submit bids on your requirement. Review each bid's:
- Compliance score (how well they match your requirements)
- Caregiver qualifications
- Proposed rates
- Agency ratings and reviews
- Any messages or clarifications from the agency

#### 3. Accept a Bid and Confirm Placement
Choose the bid that best fits your needs. Accepting locks the contract and initiates the placement process. Other bids are automatically rejected.

#### 4. Monitor Care in Real-Time
Once care begins, use your dashboard to:
- See caregiver check-ins and check-outs
- View care logs and vital recordings
- Track medication administration
- Communicate with caregivers and the agency

#### 5. Provide Feedback
After shifts are completed, rate your caregiver and the agency. Your feedback helps improve the platform and helps other families make informed decisions.

---

### For Caregivers: How to Find Work and Log Care

#### 1. Browse Available Jobs
Visit the Job Marketplace to see jobs posted by agencies. Filter by:
- Location
- Care type (elderly, pediatric, disability)
- Shift timing
- Compensation range
- Required skills

#### 2. Apply for Jobs
Submit applications for jobs that match your skills and preferences. Include a brief message explaining why you're a good fit.

#### 3. Complete Your Verification
If you haven't already, complete the 6-step verification process to become verified. Verified caregivers receive priority in job matching.

#### 4. Log Care During Shifts
When your shift begins:
1. Check in through the app (GPS and photo verification)
2. Log care activities, medications, and vitals throughout the shift
3. Add notes about the patient's condition
4. Check out at the end of your shift

#### 5. Track Your Earnings
View your earnings in real-time from the Earnings dashboard. Monitor daily income, weekly summaries, and payment dates.

---

### For Agencies: How to Manage Your Operations

#### 1. Respond to Care Requirements
Review incoming care requirements from guardians in your Requirements Inbox. Submit bids with:
- Staffing proposals
- Rates and terms
- Care plans tailored to the patient's needs

#### 2. Manage Your Caregiver Team
Use the Caregiver Roster to:
- Track all caregiver information
- Schedule shifts and manage availability
- Monitor performance and attendance
- Handle time-off requests

#### 3. Monitor Active Placements
Track all active client placements from the Placements dashboard. See:
- Shift schedules and caregiver assignments
- Real-time care logs and check-ins
- Client feedback and ratings
- Revenue and billing status

#### 4. Process Payroll
Use the automated payroll system to calculate caregiver earnings based on completed shifts. Process payouts via bKash or bank transfer with full audit trails.

---

### Using the Offline Mode

#### How It Works
When you don't have internet access:
1. Continue logging care, checking in/out, and recording vitals
2. All data saves locally on your device
3. When you reconnect, data automatically syncs to the cloud
4. No data is lost—your work is always safe

#### Best Practices
- Connect to Wi-Fi periodically to sync your data
- Complete all documentation before checking out of a shift
- The app will alert you if you have unsynced data
