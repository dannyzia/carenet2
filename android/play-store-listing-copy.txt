FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4,000 characters)
================================================================================

CareNet helps families and guardians find, book, and coordinate professional care in one place. Whether you need home support, visits, or ongoing care for a loved one, CareNet connects you with caregivers and agencies so you can move from search to confirmed care without juggling spreadsheets and endless messages.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Search and compare caregivers and care providers
• Book visits and manage schedules with clearer visibility for everyone involved
• Keep care communication in one thread—fewer missed updates across SMS and calls
• Handle billing-related flows in-app where enabled (availability may vary by region and provider)
• Stay informed with notifications for important updates (when you allow them)

FOR CAREGIVERS & STAFF

• See shifts and assignments in a structured workflow
• Document care notes and handoffs so the next person has context
• Reduce admin friction with tools designed for real-world care delivery

FOR AGENCIES

• Coordinate jobs, placements, and team workflows from a single hub
• Support transparency for families while keeping operations organized

BUILT FOR REAL LIFE

Care is personal. CareNet is designed around clarity, accountability, and respectful communication—so families spend less time coordinating and more time supporting the people they love.

NOTES

• CareNet requires an internet connection for most features.
• Features and availability can vary by account type, verification status, and region.
• CareNet does not replace emergency services. In a medical emergency, call your local emergency number.

We’re continuously improving CareNet. If something isn’t working as expected, use in-app support where available or contact us through the details on our website.
